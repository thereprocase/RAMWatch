## 2026-04-22 - [Dynamic ToolTips for Multi-State Buttons]
**Learning:** Using dynamic ToolTips for multi-state confirmation buttons is an effective way to guide users. Instead of just static 'Delete' on an 'X' button, the tooltip changing to 'Click again to confirm deletion' gives explicit instruction on the next action required, reducing confusion during destructive actions.
**Action:** When implementing multi-click confirmation buttons, always use DataTriggers to update the ToolTip along with the visual state.
